29-31 July 2016

Player Performance Hack

Join us to hack some of the most exclusive data in football and help us understand player performance in brand new ways. What will you discover?

In another world first, MCFC is opening up incredibly detailed player and match data and inviting you to create new insight, innovation, visualisations and products.

For this unique Hackathon held at the awesome Etihad Campus, data partners Opta and ChyronHego will release previously unavailable data and APIs featuring in-match events, player performance and detailed biometric tracking.

Were looking for the most talented, insightful, innovative and creative programmers, designers, visualisers and innovators. To work alongside them wed also welcome sports scientists and data analysts.

TWO TRACKS

The Premier Hack

For experienced data scientists, developers, designers and product *strikers* to get straight on the pitch - youll get full access to all the data and APIs to get stuck in over 48 hours.

The Academy

Digital innovation experts and event partner Play will facilitate a prototyping workshop for over 16s and students new to hacks. Come along and pick up new skills in an incredibly dynamic environment.

The Data

The material weve sourced is cutting edge and unfiltered, meaning youll have the best possible information and tools available to you.
